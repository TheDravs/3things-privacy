Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At 3things Journal, we believe your personal thoughts and reflections should remain private. We've designed our app with privacy as a fundamental principle, ensuring your journal entries remain solely in your control.

Data Collection and Storage

3things Journal is designed to respect your privacy completely. Here's how we handle your data:

• All journal entries and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's CoreData framework
• We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers
• Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to provide the journaling functionality within the app. This includes storing your journal entries, preferences, and app settings locally on your device.

Data Protection

Your journal entries are protected by your device's built-in security features. The app uses Apple's standard data protection mechanisms to ensure your data remains private and secure.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Apple's built-in crash reporting tools to help improve app stability. These reports do not contain any of your personal information or journal content.

Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data at all times. Through the app's settings, you can:

• Export all your journal entries
• Delete all your data
• Manage your preferences

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page and notify you through the app if there are substantial changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@3things-journal.com
